Who we are

Relocateinstyle SA

We are a boutique firm based in Lugano, specialised in guiding private individuals and families through the process of relocating to Switzerland — and specifically to the canton of Ticino.

Our approach is bespoke: every mandate is built around the client's specific needs, with no standardised solutions. We offer tailored, discreet and comprehensive support covering every aspect of life in Ticino.

Our team communicates in 10 languages, ensuring smooth, barrier-free communication at every stage of the relocation.

Our vision
To make relocating to Ticino feel not like administration, but like opening the door to a life you chose — with clarity, protection, and style.

Our story

Why in style

Relocating to Switzerland is one of life's most significant transitions. It should feel considered, supported, and aligned with who you are — not overwhelming.

Relocateinstyle was founded on the belief that relocation is not a checklist. It is a personal journey that deserves the same care you would expect from a private advisor, a family office, or a trusted friend who knows Ticino intimately.

From Lugano, we serve families, entrepreneurs and internationally mobile individuals who choose Ticino for its quality of life, its position between Italy and the Alps, and its unique Swiss-Italian character.
